I am a research associate in the department of Mathematics at Imperial College London. Before joining Imperial College, I was a postdoctoral fellow at Makerere University, Sheffield University and the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences between 2020-2023. I was a lecturer at University of Burundi, Institute of Applied Statistics after my Phd in Applied Mathematics in Benin at the Abomey-Calavi University in 2019. My current research focuses on Physics Informed Machine Learning methods with applications in air pollution and disease spread dynamics.
